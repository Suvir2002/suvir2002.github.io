About The Huawei P20 Pro

The Huawei P20 Pro is Huawei's flagship phone for 2018 coming in at CDN $1155. Huawei is changing up the design and build quality to be more competitive and promising to take down all rivals from big name manufacturers like Samsung and Apple. The Huawei P20 and Huawei P20 Pro don't look a bit like the P10 models that they replace. The design has been totally changed and you could say that it looks like the iPhone X. There's a metal core which forms the edges of the phone sandwiched between a glass front and back, which both softly curve into those edges. Imitation aside, this is probably the highest build quality that we've seen on Huawei devices so far. The Huawei P20 carries an IP53 waterproofing, while the Huawei P20 Pro bumps that up to IP67. These phones are both slim, at 7.65 and 7.8mm thick respectively. Both also tale off the 3.5mm headphone socket in the process, so be prepared to carry your dongle.

Specifications

  • CPU/GPU: Kirin 970 Octa-core (4x2.4 GHz Cortex-A73 & 4x1.8 GHz Cortex-A53) with Mali-G72 MP12 GPU
  • Display: AMOLDED 6.1 inch 1080 x 2240 display (408 ppi density)
  • Storage: 128 GB of non-expandable storage
  • RAM: 6 GB RAM
  • Camera Primary: Triple: 40 MP (f/1.8, 27 mm, 1/1.7", OIS) + 20 MP (f/1.6, 27 mm) + 8 MP (f/2.4, 80 mm), Leica optics, 3x optical zoom, phase detection and laser autofocus
  • Camera Secondary: 24 MP, autofocus, f/2.0, 1080p
