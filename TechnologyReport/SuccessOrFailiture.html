Success Or Failiure

I think this product will have some success but is handicapped with not being one of the very well-known brands in North America. Also, the phone has Google apps but with Huawei variants and users are not able to modify or remove these apps. The phone comes in 128 GB only and there are many users who may want less storage and a lower price. To overcome this, Huawei could offer models will less storage and lower RAM so that the lower price would enable them to capture a greater market share.
