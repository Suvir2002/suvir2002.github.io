Dubai


Tourist Attractions

  • Burj Khalifa

    • The Burj Khlaifa is a bulding located in the United Arab Emrites. It is the tallest bullding in the world standing a massive 160 stories (828m). Once you have reached the top you can enjoy the highest outdoor observatory in the world.



  • Burj Al Arab

    • The Burj Al Arab is a luxury hotel located in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It is the third tallest hotel in the world (39% of its total height is made up of non-occupiable space). Burj Al Arab stands on an artificial island 280 m from Jumeirah beach and is connected to the mainland by a private curving bridge. The shape of the structure is designed to resemble the sail of a ship. It has a helipad near the roof at a height of 210 m above ground.



  • The Dubai Mall

    • The Dubai Mall is the largest mall in the world with a toal area of 1,124,000 square meters. It has 1200 shops inside and even a glass aquirium where you can view over 33,000 animals, you can even dive with the sharks! It also has it's own olympic sized skating rink and theme park.



  • The Dubai Fountain

    • The Dubai Fountain is the world's largest choreographed fountain system set on the 30-acre manmade Burj Khalifa Lake, at the center of Downtown Dubai in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It was desgined by WET Design a California based company, it is 275m (902ft) long and shoots water up to 152.4m (500ft).


  • Dubai Aquarium and Underwater Zoo

    • The aquarium is one of the largest suspended aquariums in the world having a 10-million litre tank. It houses thousands of aquatic animals, having over 140 species. Over 300 Sharks and Rays live in this tank, including the largest collection of Sand Tiger Sharks in the world.
