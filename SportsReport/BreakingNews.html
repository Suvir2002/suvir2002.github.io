NBA Star Wins Oscar

At Sunday night's 90th annual Academy Awards, retired NBA star Kobe Bryant wins Oscar for his short animated film "Dear Basketball". After winning the Oscar Kobe said, "I don't know if it's possible," Bryant retorted. "I mean, as basketball players, we are really supposed to shut up and dribble. But I am glad we do a little bit more than that."

Getting Rid of One-And-Done

In the wake of the pay-for-play scandal rocking college basketball and the NCAA, Adam Silver and the NBA are considering removing the 'one-and-done' age limit rule for players who want to enter the NBA draft among other strategies to get more involved with youth basketball on a global scale. We are not sure when or even if this will but it is likely it will.
