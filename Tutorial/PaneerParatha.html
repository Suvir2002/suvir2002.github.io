How To Make A Paneer Paratha

11 Steps To Make Your Own Paneer Paratha

Step: 1

In the picture are all the ingredients you will need to make your own paneer paratha.

Step: 2

Take one cup of flour and add ½ cup of water to make it into a dough. Also put a little vegetable oil while kneading. Keep it aside for ½ an hour.

Step: 3

Grate 5 small cubes of paneer.

Step: 4

Dice a ¼ onion into small pieces.

Step: 5

Dice ½ a serano pepper into fine pieces.

Step: 6

Chop a few coriander leaves.

Step: 7

Heat some vegetable oil in a pan.

Step: 8

Roll the dough into a circular shape with a rolling pin and put paneer, onions, serrano pepper, coriander, some cumin seeds and finally salt and pepper to taste on top.

Step: 9

Now fold it in and then roll it into a flat circular shape again.

Step: 10

Fry it in the pan until it is golden brown, don’t forget to flip it. It will burn if you don’t.

Step: 11

The paneer paratha is ready to serve. Serve it in a plate with a wedge of butter on top.
