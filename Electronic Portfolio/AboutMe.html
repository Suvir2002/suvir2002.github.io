About Me

My name is Jeff Green. I moved to Redmond, Washington, United States of America from Surrey, British Columbia. I completed my Undergrad Degree in Business from Simon Fraser University and completed my Masters in Software Systems from the University of British Columbia in Vancouver. With my passion for computers, entering the field of Software Development was a natural fit for me. Obtaining a job with Microsoft Corporation as a Software Engineer completed my dream for me. In a short span of five years, I am now an Architect with Microsoft Corporation. In my free time, you will find me on the basketball court with friends playing friendly matches or biking around Seattle and experiencing the various culinary delights the city has to offer.
